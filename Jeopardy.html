Social Studies Question

Put these in order from earliest to latest:

(A) Heian period - 平安時代

(B) Yayoi period - 弥生時代

(C) Asuka period - 飛鳥時代

Answer

(B) Yayoi period - 弥生時代 - 300 BC to AD 300 (about)

(C) Asuka period - 飛鳥時代 - AD 538 to 710 (or 592 to 645)

(A) Heian period - 平安時代 - AD 794 to 1185
